Bell Bottom Movie Review 


	Bell Bottom is a 2021 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film directed by Ranjit M Tewari.
	
	It stars Akshay Kumar as the lead character, with Lara Dutta, Vaani Kapoor and Huma Qureshi in other prominent roles.
	
	ell Bottom is inspired from real life hijacking events in India by Khalistani separatists during the 1980s, such as the Indian Airlines Flight 423, 405 and 421 hijackings.
